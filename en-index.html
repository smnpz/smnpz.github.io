Anacronauta

Implemented in Unity for the project of UNICAM - Informatica x Gioco = Fantasia + Regole Beta under review

Anacronauta: an adventure that mixes anachronism (chronological inaccuracy) with nautical exploration through time.

Plot:
The protagonist, during a museum visit on the anniversary of the Unification of Italy, is transported to 1861, to Teano, where an accident causes a temporal paradox. Armed only with the hilt of Garibaldi's sword, he becomes immune to the effects of the paradox and must rebuild the sword to restore the temporal order.

Synopsis:
"Anacronauta" is a roguelike role-playing game where the player explores different Italian epochs, freeing regions from temporal anomalies. They must find sword fragments and receive advice from enlightened minds of the past to solve the paradox and complete the unification of Italy.

Main Settings:
The game features 8+1 main zones, each in a different epoch, plus a macro area outside the temporal bubbles.

Endings:
The player can achieve two endings: in the first, they return to the present after reassembling the sword and shaking hands with Garibaldi; in the second, they destroy the sword and decide to directly influence the course of history.

Characters:
Among the characters present are Fiore dei Liberi, a fencing master, and other historical figures who offer advice and teachings to the protagonist.

Progression:
The player traverses various zones, unlocking time-linked magical abilities and facing optional bosses before returning to Teano to complete the adventure.
